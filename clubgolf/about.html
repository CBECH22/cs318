What We Are and What We Stand For

We are a club that is open to any golfer who wants to play golf for fun and/or competatively. We want every golfer who loves the game to be able to compete. Anyone who joins will be able to try out for the team. Even if you don't make the team you are more than welcome to play golf with our membership that is active with clubs throughout Eau Claire.
